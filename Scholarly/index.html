Abstract

GaMa is a website that is desired for people all around the glob that wants to play different types of video games, easy and fast without any sophisticated steps and last but not least, the registered clients will be able to register in tournaments for specific games.

Introduction

As we all know, these days, the video games are gainning more and more popularity. Back in the day, people had to go in the shops in order to buy the video game that they wanted. That means they had to wait for the game to be in the shop, to see if that certain game is in stock and so on. That's why the GaMa website is useful, by using it people can buy their game instantly, receiving a virtual copy of it, without waiting.

The purpose of this website is to:

  • Make it easy to play diffrent games with people all around the glob by joining tourneys.
  • Easy to choose.
  • Easy to use
  • See all of your tournaments in one place.
